Boba House

Vegetarian and Vegan Friendly!

Discover the essence of Vietnamese cuisine and culinary creativity at Boba House, a beloved gem nestled in Greensboro's eclectic dining scene. Located on bustling Tate Street, this charming eatery offers a delightful fusion of traditional flavors and modern twists, making it a go-to spot for foodies and bubble tea enthusiasts alike. Be sure to check the time as hours vary from 11:30AM - 3:00PM and 5:00PM - 9:00PM on most days. They also do takeout!

Supreme Spring Rolls

2 rolls filled with vermicelli, lettuce, bean sprouts, basil leaves, cilantro, and wrapped in translucent rice paper. Served w/ peanut sauce. Available with tofu, avocado, “ribs”, or “shrimp”.

Price: $7

Crab Sandwhich

Delicious "crab" on a bun with your choice of side.

Price: $8.99

Pad Thai

Flat rice noodle stir-fried w/ green onion, bean sprout, and your choice of “meat” or vegetables and topped with crushed peanuts.

Price: $8.99

Tate Street Coffee

Cozy Place to study and enjoy coffee!

Nestled in the heart of Greensboro's vibrant Tate Street, Tate Street Coffee exudes a cozy charm that's perfect for those seeking respite from the bustling city. Indulge in their signature Iced Chai Latte, a refreshing blend of chai tea and your choice of milk, or savor their delectable array of pastries while soaking in the laid-back ambiance. Tate street hours go form 6:30AM - 6PM most days, be sure to check their website for my accurate times! They do dine-in or carry-out!

Iced Chai latte

Chai tea on ice with your choice of milk or milk substitute.

Price: $4

Old Town Draught House

Finger licking good!

Welcome to Old Town Draught House, a lovely pub in Greensboro's historic district. Whether you're craving classic pub fare or in the mood for something a bit more adventurous, their menu offers a tantalizing selection of options to satisfy every palate. From their mouthwatering Chicken Tenders, beer-battered to perfection and served with honey mustard, to their indulgent Loaded Fries topped with BBQ brisket and cheddar, each dish is crafted with care and served with a side of Southern hospitality. Old Town Draught House has the same schedule every day from 11AM - 11PM.
